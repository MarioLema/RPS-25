Rock Paper Scissors 25 Emoji Edition

Based on the classic Rock Paper Scissors game this version features 25 different options and 300 different outcomes. The game was created by David C. Lovelace and you can check it in his website here. I have created this game with vanilla Javascript and emojis instead of hand gestures. Just click on your favourite emoji to start playing. Enjoy!
